Our Story

Founded in 2021 by University of Arizona students Ali, Eyad, and Justin, TechEdgeLearning emerged from their shared experiences in computer science. Recognizing the challenges and complexities of the subject, they united to establish a platform dedicated to supporting computer science students at the University of Arizona, ensuring no one navigates the academic journey without guidance. Our aim is simple: make computer science comprehensible and accessible to all.

What We Offer

1. One-on-One Tutoring: Personalized sessions tailored to the unique needs of each student. Whether you're struggling with algorithms, data structures, or any other computer science topic, our team of dedicated tutors is here to help!

2. Group Sessions: Collaboration fosters creativity. Our group sessions are designed to be interactive, allowing students to work together on challenging problems, encouraging diverse thought and innovative solutions.

3. Hands-On Coding Workshops: Beyond the theory, we believe in the power of practice. Our monthly coding workshops challenge students to implement what they've learned in real-world scenarios.
